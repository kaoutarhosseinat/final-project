Who Are We ?
IXINA is the specialist in functional and designer fitted kitchens suitable for all lifestyles. Our brand is part of the FBD International group, the leading European franchisor of made-to-measure fitted kitchens. For more than 40 years, IXINA has been nourished by the expertise and solidity of the group in order to guarantee you the best HUMAN-QUALITY-PRICE ratio.
Our International Presence
Created in 1971 in Belgium, the success of ixina quickly overflowed its borders to come to France and then internationally. With approximately 30 store openings per year and a network of nearly 250 stores, ixina has become the worldwide benchmark for German quality, functional and trendy fitted kitchens. Supported by a strong group and by the expertise of men and women committed to your satisfaction, ixina continues its development in the four corners of the world to earn every day a little more the trust of demanding customers, such as you.
Our store concept
Our ixina Expert Advisors welcome you to a warm, friendly and transparent space. In the central part of the showroom, you will find an open space in which you can discover the richness of our assortment, touch and compare materials, learn about current trends and choose with your Advisor the combinations and features that suit you. For even more inspiration, on the outskirts of this space, we invite you to discover our staging of fitted kitchens that are functional, realistic and stylish.
